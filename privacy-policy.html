This Privacy Policy describes how OopsLater ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

OopsLater is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Data We Store Locally

  • Timer Data: Your decision descriptions, timer durations, and expiration dates
  • Response History: Your Yes/No responses to expired timers
  • App Settings: Your notification preferences and app configuration
  • Notification Data: Local notification schedules for timer expiration
Important: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device and is under your complete control.

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally, we use your information only to:

  • Display your active timers and countdown information
  • Show your decision history and response patterns
  • Send local notifications when timers expire
  • Maintain your app preferences and settings

Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely on your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • We do not have access to your personal information
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures

Third-Party Services

OopsLater does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect your personal data. The app operates entirely offline and locally.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • You can reset your data through the app's settings
  • You have full access to all data stored on your device
  • You can export your data through your device's backup features

Children's Privacy

OopsLater is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
